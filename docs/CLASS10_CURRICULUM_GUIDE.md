### Mathematics (100 Marks)
- **High Priority Chapters**: Real Numbers, Quadratic Equations, Triangles, Trigonometry, Surface Areas & Volumes
- **Medium Priority**: Polynomials, Coordinate Geometry, Statistics, Circles
- **Exam Pattern**: Section A (1 mark), B (2 marks), C (3 marks), D (5 marks)

### Science (100 Marks)  
- **Physics**: Light, Electricity, Magnetic Effects
- **Chemistry**: Acids & Bases, Metals & Non-metals, Carbon Compounds
- **Biology**: Life Processes, Control & Coordination, Heredity & Evolution
- **Exam Focus**: Diagrams, chemical equations, numerical problems

### English (100 Marks)
- **Literature**: First Flight (Prose & Poetry), Footprints without Feet
- **Grammar**: Tenses, Voice, Reported Speech, Determiners
- **Writing**: Letters, Articles, Stories, Notices
- **Marks Distribution**: Reading (26), Writing (30), Grammar (16), Literature (28)

### Social Science (100 Marks)
- **Geography**: Resources, Agriculture, Industries, Transportation
- **History**: Nationalism, Freedom Movement
- **Political Science**: Power Sharing, Federalism, Democracy
- **Economics**: Development, Money & Credit
